About Me

Biography

I am an Electrical and Electronics Engineer from Barisal Engineering College with a strong academic background and hands-on research experience. My undergraduate thesis focused on creating an Explainable AI framework for non-invasive blood glucose prediction, which has been accepted for publication. I possess a diverse technical skill set, including proficiency in engineering software, programming, and design tools. My leadership roles in various college clubs have honed my project management, team collaboration, and communication skills. I am eager to apply my knowledge and skills to a challenging research role in the engineering field.

Research Interests

My undergraduate work establishes a foundation for advanced Master's and PhD research in intelligent systems. I am actively seeking opportunities to explore:

  • Explainable AI (XAI): Advancing model interpretability using techniques like SHAP and LIME for trustworthy non-invasive health monitoring.
  • Robotics & Autonomous Systems: Developing algorithms for complex sensor data fusion and real-time processing in autonomous environments.
  • IoT & Embedded Systems: Engineering robust hardware systems utilizing microcontrollers (ESP32) and protocols (MQTT) for smart telemetry.
