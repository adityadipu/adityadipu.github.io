AI Blood Glucose Predictor

An Explainable AI (XAI) framework for non-invasive blood glucose prediction and hypoglycemia detection.

View Live Demo View on GitHub
Back to All Projects

Project Overview

The Research: This project is the culmination of my undergraduate thesis. The primary goal was to develop an innovative AI model capable of accurately predicting blood glucose levels using non-invasive data, such as data from wearable sensors.

The "Explainable" Core: A key part of the research was not just to create an accurate model, but a trustworthy one. I implemented Explainable AI (XAI) techniques to interpret the model's decisions, making it possible to understand *why* it made a specific prediction. This is critical for applications in medical technology.

Technologies Used

Python TensorFlow Scikit-learn Pandas Matplotlib XAI (LIME/SHAP) Gradio

Publication

The findings of this research were accepted for publication at the International Conference on Multidisciplinary Computer Science, Electrical, Business & Literature (ICMCEL 2025).

"A Feature-Optimized Approach for Blood Glucose Forecasting Using Explainable AI (XAI)"
